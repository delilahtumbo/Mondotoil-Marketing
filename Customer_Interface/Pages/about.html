Goal


As a young local Papua New Guinean web developer, I develop this user friendly ecommerce website to help Papua New Guineans market their products Online, these products can be locally made and owned. After seeing that many shops that are owned by Papua New Guineans are advertising their product using social media, but are not selling anythings, this website aims to create a flexiable and reliable virtual site that shops can sell their products locally and globally.
